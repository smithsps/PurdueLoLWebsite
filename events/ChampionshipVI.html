Purdue League of Legends Group is proud to present Purdue Championship VI. Last semester, Team Omega Magma went on as the semester champions! With this semester looming can they have a repeat? Or will someone rise from the ashes? Come join us in some competitive fun!

If you have any questions, contact an officer using the Contact Us page or post it in the PurdueLoL Facebook page!
